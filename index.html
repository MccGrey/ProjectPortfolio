Frequently asked questions

These are some of the questions I get asked oftenly

  • What is your hourly rate?

    My rate usually depends on the scope of the project.

  • What services do you offer?

    I provide branding, logo design, social media graphics, print materials, website graphics, motion design, and custom illustrations depending on client needs.

  • How do you price your work?

    Pricing can be hourly or project-based. I usually prefer project packages so clients know the full cost upfront. Rates depend on the complexity, deliverables, and timeline.

  • How long does a project take?

    Timelines vary—simple designs may take a few days, while full branding packages can take 2–4 weeks. I’ll always give you a timeline before we start.

  • What if I don’t like the first draft?

    Revisions are included in my packages. I’ll refine the design based on your feedback until we achieve the result you’re happy with.

  • What do you need from me to get started?

    I’ll need a project brief (your goals, preferences, and references), brand assets (if any), and any text or content you’d like included.
